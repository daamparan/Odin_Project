Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a white sauce and meat-based red sauce on a flat expanded pasta sheet that traditionally is made with Parmigiano-Reggiano a.k.a Parmesan Cheese. Much like with everything and all other foods, lasagne has gone through many different iterations. The recipe that will be outlined below will try to stick to the traditional recipe as much as possible. Logical re- placements for some ingredients will be made and noted as the recipe is outlined.

Ingredients

  • 1lb Lasagna noodles/sheets
  • 10 oz shredded mozzarella
  • 2 Tbsp grated Parmiagiano Reggiano cheese
  • Meat Sauce
  • Bechmael Sauce

Meat Sauce Ingredients

  • 4 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 4 Cloves of Garlic
  • 2 Tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 lb of ground beef
  • 42 oz Italian tomato puree
    • Can be whole tomatoes, pureeed
  • Kosher or Sea Salt
  • Pepper
  • Fresh Basil

Bechmael Sauce Ingredients

  • 3 oz of Butter
  • 4 oz of Flour
  • 24 oz of warm Milk, whole is best
  • Dash of nutmeg
  • Kosher or Sea salt
  • White Pepper

Steps

  1. Set oven temperature to 400 ℉
  2. Make the Meat Sauce
    1. In a large pot combine olive oil, garlic, and parsley over medium high heat.
    2. As the garlic begins to brown increase the heat
    3. Add the ground beef
    4. Break up the beef
    5. Sprinkle about 1/2 tsp of salt
    6. As the beef begins to dry up add the tomato puree
    7. Add some more salt if needed and lower the heat
    8. Simmer the sauce for about an hour and season with salt and pepper to your liking
    9. Once the sauce is ready add the basil leaves
    10. Remove about a cup of the plain sauce, leave the ground beef in the pot for the top layer
  3. Make the Bechamel Sauce (Can be made while the sauce is simmering)
    1. Melt butter in a pot then add flour
    2. Keep stirring to cook the flour for about 5 minutes
    3. Pour in a little of the milk and stire to incorporate
    4. Continue stirring whilst adding milk a little at a time
    5. Continue adding as the composition gets more sauce like
    6. Once all the milk has been added season iwth 1/2 tsp salt, dash nutmeg, and 1/4 tsp white pepper
    7. Taste and adjust to liking
    8. Set aside if with a lid if not using right away
  4. Boil the noodles in salted water whilst moving them so they do not stick
  5. Remove the noodles from the heat 5 minutes before the instructed time
  6. Reserve about 2 cups of the pasta water
  7. Refill the pot with cold water just to cover the noodles
  8. Assemble the Lasagna
    1. Put thin layer of the red sauce (with no meat)
    2. Add three pieces of lasagna lengthwise and top with the bechamel sauce
    3. Add the meat sauce but not too much
    4. Sprinkle some mozzarella
    5. Top with more lasgna sheets
    6. Cut them to 9 ″
